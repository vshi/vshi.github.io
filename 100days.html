100 Days of Design

In February of 2016, three friends and I decided to challenge ourselves to create 100 days of design. We did this because all four of us recognized we could be pushing ourselves harder - to create more, faster, and better. Creativity may be born from inspiration and spontaneity, but it is honed by discipline and practice, practice, practice.

As of now, the project is still ongoing. If you'd like, check in on our progress at @kvltklassic on Instagram.

We're now a quarter of the way through the project. Nobody is more than a few days behind yet!
